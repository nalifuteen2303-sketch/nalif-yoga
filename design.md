---

## 1. Visual Identity

### Aesthetic
Spiritual, ethereal, calming — a modern spa/yoga aesthetic emphasizing serenity, balance, and inner beauty. Image-heavy with strong emphasis on human form (yoga poses), natural textures, and warm earthy tones.

### Color Palette
- **Primary background:** White / off-white / cream
- **Accent:** Earthy sage green, warm neutrals, muted naturals (typical of yoga/wellness themes)
- **Text:** Dark grey / near-black for body copy
- **UI highlights:** Soft overlays, subtle gradients on hero/CTA banners
- **Sale/discount badges:** Warm contrast color (likely red/orange)

### Typography
- **Display headings:** Stylized serif/script fonts for words like `KRIYA`, `Eternal Love`, `Live towards Eternity` — large, expressive, brand-defining
- **Section headings (H2/H3):** Clean serif or modern sans-serif
- **Body copy:** Sans-serif, comfortable line-height for readability
- **Hierarchy:** Mixed sizing — display headings oversized, sub-headlines medium, body text small-to-medium

### Layout Principles
- Full-width sections stacked vertically
- Centered content blocks with generous padding
- Alternating image/text layouts for storytelling
- Grid-based modules for repeating content (team, products, videos)
- Boxed and full-width variants across sections
